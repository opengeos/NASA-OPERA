# DIST

## Overview

The **Land Surface Disturbance (DIST)** product suite identifies vegetation
and land-cover disturbances caused by natural and anthropogenic events, such
as wildfire, deforestation, agricultural conversion, and urban development.
DIST supports OPERA's surface-disturbance mission objective with frequent,
analysis-ready disturbance maps derived from optical satellite imagery.

## Product level & distribution

- Processing level: Level-3
- DAAC: LP DAAC
- Project: NASA OPERA (JPL)

## Sub-products

- **DIST-ALERT-HLS** — near-real-time vegetation disturbance alerts generated
  per HLS acquisition.
- **DIST-ANN-HLS** — annual summary of vegetation disturbance aggregated over
  a calendar year.

## Applications

- Deforestation and forest-degradation monitoring
- Wildfire progression and burn-scar mapping
- Agricultural expansion and land-use change
- Inputs for carbon accounting and biodiversity studies

## Inputs

- Harmonized Landsat Sentinel-2 (HLS) surface reflectance
- Historical land-cover and vegetation reference layers

## Coverage

Global land coverage, with alert cadence driven by the HLS revisit frequency.
